Volume II — Idea Theory and the Social Sciences

Scope. Interpret the nine theorems in the social sciences: norms, institutions, opinion dynamics, cultural transmission, social conflict, and collective action.

Background from the Literature

Boyd & Richerson (1985) and Henrich (2015) treat culture as inherited information transmitted along chains, naturally modelled by T7 (transmission). Sperber's (1996) epidemiology of representations corresponds to T1 (composition). Granovetter's (1978) threshold models of collective behaviour and DeGroot's (1974) opinion dynamics motivate T2 (resonance pairing). Bourdieu's (1977) habitus and field theory provide a setting for T6 (conjugation as reframing under social position) and T8 (structural equivalence between practices).
